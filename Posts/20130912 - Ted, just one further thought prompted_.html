Ted, just one further thought prompted by this post: We talked about the notion of making the underlying triple structure of the Exhibit database more visible/accessible to users. It would be wildly cool if Exhibit could not only natively accept JSON-LD (or really any other RDF extended/quad format, especially TRIG), but also had a built-in mechanism for displaying named graphs. (Think "Grouped as Sorted")
